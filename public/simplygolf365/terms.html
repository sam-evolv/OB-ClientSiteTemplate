4. Venue, space and power

You're responsible for providing a suitable venue and safe access for setup and takedown. The dome needs:

  • Outdoor (standard dome): a 5m × 5m footprint, 6m × 6m recommended; large dome: 7m × 7m footprint, 8m × 8m recommended.
  • Indoor venues: at least 10m × 5m of floor space and a minimum 3m ceiling height.
  • A standard 13A power supply within reach of the setup area.
  • Reasonable vehicle access for loading in and out, and a flat, stable surface.

If you're not sure a venue works, send us a photo before you book and we'll confirm the fit. If the venue on the day doesn't meet these requirements and we can't safely set up, the booking is treated as a customer cancellation under Section 5.
